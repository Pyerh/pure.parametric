Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Do I need to own Rhino or Grasshopper before starting?
No. Rhino offers a free 90-day evaluation, and Grasshopper is included with Rhino. We'll set everything up together in the first lesson if needed.
What experience level do I need?
None. I teach complete beginners as well as professionals. The first 30-minute trial helps me understand your level and goals so I can tailor the curriculum.
Online or in person?
Both. Online lessons are over Zoom with screen sharing — works just as well as in person. If you're in Bangkok, we can meet in person at agreement place.
What language do you teach in?
English or Thai — your choice. Just let me know which you prefer when booking.
What if I miss or want to reschedule a lesson?
Free reschedule with 24 hours notice. Within 24 hours, lesson is forfeited unless there's an emergency.
How does payment work?
Pay per course via PromptPay (Thai bank transfer). No subscription, no auto-renewal. Refund available before the second lesson if it's not a fit.
